This story is about a character named Leo who is born in a world very much different than the one we live in currently. Although our worlds share many similarities, it is only through reading you will find the difference. A world where one single mutation changed everything; animals, structures, commodity's are all up in question and discovery. However with a different world comes a different set of challenges. This story is about a boy named Leo becoming a man, but not just any man, a Revolutionary man, in a time of Evolution. It's through this story that one sees loss from all sides of a front, and also, gain? We often forget as a society what were the installments of what we have, the birth right of freedom and opportunity. You'll find while reading this story that even though the story takes place 500 years ahead into the future, in some way, we learn more about the past and ultimately ourselves as people. We see what it TAKES.. loss or gain, for a world to carve into beauty or.. the exact opposite.
